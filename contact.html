FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need an appointment?

Walk-ins are welcome, but we recommend booking an appointment to guarantee your preferred time slot and barber.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, bank transfers, and all major mobile payment platforms (POS available).

How long does a typical service take?

A standard haircut takes 30-45 minutes. Combo services take about 60 minutes. VIP experience is 90 minutes.

Can I request a specific barber?

Absolutely! You can request your preferred barber when booking online or calling ahead.
