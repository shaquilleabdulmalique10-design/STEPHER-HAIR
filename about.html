About Us

Crafting excellence in grooming since 2012

Our Story

Crafting Excellence Since 2012

At Stepher Hair, we believe grooming is an art form. Our team of master barbers brings over 12 years of combined experience, delivering precision cuts and premium styling that elevate your confidence.

From classic cuts to modern fades, beard sculpting to hair coloring, we use only the finest products and techniques to ensure you leave looking and feeling your absolute best.

Located in the heart of Ijebu-Ode, Ogun State, at Molipa Express front of i-fix complex, we've become the go-to destination for discerning gentlemen who demand excellence in grooming.
